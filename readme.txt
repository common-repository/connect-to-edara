== Description ==

About Edara ERP
Stay on top of your business Run your entire business including finances, sales, and inventory with access at any moment with cloud-connected functionality. Edara ERP fits any type of business whether it's a complete E-commerce business, a retail store, or distribution, you name it. Edara ERP connects all your business operations in a single, integrated cloud solution. It helps you better manage your finances, operations, and inventory through one comprehensive solution.

How does Edara ERP work?
Edara accompanies you throughout your trading journey. With our modules (sales, accounting, warehouses, and purchases), you can take care of all your operations. The modules allow you to manage, keep track of, and analyze all your business transactions in a smart and easy way to realize your potential. Stay alert with Edara's endless reports.

SALES: Maintain your sales order workflow with Edara’s sales module through monitoring Sales approvals cycle In common Point-Of-Sale model Moreover, the module allows you to schedule all the recurring sales orders to be made ‎automatically at the time you specify. So you don’t need to do it ‎yourself anymore.
PURCHASES: Sit back and monitor your purchases and their workflow. For further monitoring, the module provides a report on every: Item, transaction, customer, and purchaser, so you are always updated with the latest progress. And for a better experience, Edara links between purchase orders and inventory control.
ACCOUNTING: Get real-time data records with a set of beautifully designed and managed instant-‎massive financial reports.‎ The accounting module offers Chart of accounts Unlimited accounts grouping levels Cost Centers Detailed trial balance Financial Statements (Income Statement, Cash Flow and Balance Sheet) Accounts Receivables Accounts Payables Cash and Collection Management with complete reports for each. Unleash the power of income ‎growth for your organization with financial reports for ‎every cost center.
WAREHOUSES: Be aware of any inventory shortage before it happens. The module offers a more comprehensive perception of your inventory movements with its complete set of inventory reports. It allows you to manage warehouses of all sizes and stock items.
Business types
Edara fits many business types and sizes. Whether you are a startup or a lifestyle/family business, you run a complete E-commerce business or traditional brick and mortar, Edara is your best accounting solution.

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* PHP 7.2 or greater is recommended
* MySQL 5.6 or greater is recommended

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option -- WordPress will handles the file transfer, and you won’t need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Edara, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu, and click “Add New.”
 
In the search field type “Edara” then click “Search Plugins.” Once you’ve found us,  you can view details about it such as the point release, rating, and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by! Click “Install Now,” and WordPress will take it from there.

= Manual installation =

Manual installation method requires downloading the Edara plugin and uploading it to your web server via your favorite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/#manual-plugin-installation).

= Updating =

Automatic updates should work smoothly, but we still recommend you back up your site.
